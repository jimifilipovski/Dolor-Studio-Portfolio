Carvey by Inventables

  • Identity
  • Visual Design
  • Website
  • Kickstarter Assets

Carvey is a 3D carving machine that allows you to make quality objects out of a variety of materials including wood, metal and plastic. First, design your product using our software, next choose your material, then click carve. Carvey is the next step in 3D manufacturing. Working with Inventables I designed the logo, branding, product page and kickstarter assets.

↑ Carvey is a Chicago product. The industrial design team MNML and Inventables are all based here. Naturally we needed a font designed in Chicago so we choose Alright Sans by Jackson Cavanaugh.
